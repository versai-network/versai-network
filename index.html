Versai Network

The Versai Forums is the home to talk about literally anything you would like! Whether to apply for staff or make a suggestion, every post counts!

Versai is a 100% non-profit server, meaning that the money we receive via donations goes towards covering costs of plugin development and server hosting. Ranks have a fair price and will make you stand out from the croud!

Through voting, you help us to increase a higher rank in the Minecraft server lists - and if this didn't convince you, let us present you our 24 hour voter rank that gives you the same kits as users with paid ranks!

Stay up to date with the latest announcements, engage with other players and make friends by joining our Discord server.
